About

This is an interactive dashboard where you can compare anime characters from different series. Each character has stats for Strength, Speed, IQ, Durability, Energy, and Combat skills.

Use the radar charts and bar graphs to visualize how characters stack up against each other. Turn on Compare Mode to see two characters side-by-side and find out who would win in a battle.
